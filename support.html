Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

How can I report a bug or issue?

You can report bugs or issues through our live chat or by submitting a detailed description via the contact form below.

What platforms does HY-rage support?

HY-rage supports PC, consoles, and mobile platforms, providing a seamless gaming experience across devices.

How do I join a clan?

Navigate to the Clans section and browse available clans. You can send join requests directly through clan profiles.

Where can I find tournaments and events?

Check the Events page regularly for upcoming tournaments, special events, and community challenges.
